Active Reader

Reading is an excellent exercise for improving your knowledge of a foreign language. ActiveReader is a Google Chrome extension that helps you to learn more while reading by removing random words from webpages and then asking you to fill in the blanks. This forces you to pay more attention to what your reading thereby improving retention of the finer details of a foreign language such as gender and conjugation.

Chrome Web Store

Technologies

  • Backbone.js
